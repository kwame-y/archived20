# Keywords/Diagrams
- **[[Urbanization]]**: the movement of *people* to, and the clustering of people in, towns and [[cities]]
	- Most [[cities|cities]] start from [[rural]] areas, especially [[Agricultural Hearth|agricultural hearths]]
- **[[Urban Sprawl]]**: the tendency of **[[cities]]** to grow *outward*

			- [[Cities]] - 100,000 - 1,000,000

					- [[Villages]] - 100 - 1,000
						- [[Hamlets]] <100
