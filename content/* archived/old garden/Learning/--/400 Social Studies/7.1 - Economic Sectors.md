> There are 5 types of economic sectors
# [[Primary Sector|Primary Sectors]]
>Economic activity concerned with the direct extraction of [[natural resources]] from the environment, such as [[agriculture]], mining, farming, ranching, etc.
# [[Secondary Sector|Secondary Sectors]]
> Economic activity involving the process of raw materials and their transformation into finished industrial products.
> - Stone Cutters
> - Construction
> - Assembly Lines
# [[Tertiary Sector|Tertiary Sectors]]
> Economic activity associated with the provision of services, such as transportation/communication, producer services and consumer services.(i.e. selling the product)
> - Retail Workers
> - Service Workers
# [[Quaternary Sector|Quaternary Sectors]]
>[[Service sector]] industries concerned with the collections, processing, and manipulation of information capital.
> - Doctors??
> - Insurance
> - CPA
> - Accountant
> 	- Tax Accountant
> - Judge
> - Detective
# [[Quinary Sector|Quinary Sectors]]
>[[Service sector]] focusing on highest levels of management in business, government, education and science, often with [[global scale]] impact.
> - President
> - Jef Bezos
> - All the Rich People
> - CEOs
