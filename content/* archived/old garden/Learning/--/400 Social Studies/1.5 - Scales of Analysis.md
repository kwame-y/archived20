# [[Scale]]
- We can say that maps are either [[Large Scale]] or [[Small Scale]].
- A [[Small Scale]] map shows a large area, but with minimal detail. **([[Small Scale]], few detail)**
	- shows **LARGE area** with **SMALL amounts of data**. Zoomed OUT. BIG # on map. (i.e 1/94,000,000)
- A [[Large Scale]] map shows a small area, but with incredible detail. **([[Large Scale]], lots of details)** ^249651
	- show **SMALL area** with **LARGE amounts of data**. Zoomed IN. Small # on map. (i.e 1,300,000)
