# [[Agriculture]]
## [[Intensive Agriculture]]
### [[Market Gardening]]
- The *relatively* **small scale** production of fruits, vegetables, and flowers as cash crops, frequently sold directly to consumers at farmers markets
- Crops are grown on a small area of [[Arable Land|land]], typically from under one acre to a few acres, and sometimes in greenhouses
![[Market_Gardening_-_2019.jpeg]]
![[Farm-from-above-only-1.3-acres-in-production-708x466-1.jpg]]
### [[Plantations]]
- Large-scale, commercial farming in which a single crop is grown for the *entire* year
- Typically found in the tropical climate zones
![[rice.jpeg]]
### [[Mixed Crop-Livestock Farming]]
- A type of farming which involves both the growing of crops *and* the raising of livestock.
