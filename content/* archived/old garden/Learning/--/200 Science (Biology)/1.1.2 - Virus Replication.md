# Viral Replication
- [[Virus|Viruses]] have **two** different ways of reproducing
	- [[Lytic Cycle|Lytic]] → A [[Virus]] **quickly** replicates and creates disease symptoms (DAYS)
	- [[Lysogenic Cycle|Lysogenic]] → A [[Virus]] infects [[Cell|cells]] and remains **dormant** while being copied (YEARS)
