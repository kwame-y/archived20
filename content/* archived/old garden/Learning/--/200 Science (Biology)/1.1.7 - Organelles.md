# Organelles & Functions
## [[Cytoplasmic Membrane|Cell Membrane]]
- Found in all cells
- Also called the *[[1.2.4 - Lipids#^868bba|phospholipid]] bilayer* or *plasma membrane*
- **Barrier that regulates what enters & exits the cell**
- Composed of 3 of the 4 biomolecules
	- Carbohydrate ID tags
	- Protein transport molecules
	- Lipid Bilayer
![[Cell membrane.jpeg]]
## [[Cell Wall]]
- Found in [[1.1.4 -  Viruses vs. Prokaryotes#Prokaryotes|Prokaryotes]] & [[1.1.5 - Prokaryotes vs. Eukaryotes#Eukaryotes|Eukaryotes]]
- **Made of cellulose (carbohydrate) in plants**
- Added layer of protection
- **Gives cells strong, rigid structure**
![[Plant_cell_wall_diagram-en.svg-58a8766c3df78c345bdc5df3.png]]
## [[Centrioles]]
- Found in **Animal Cells**
- Paired
- Located in [[Cytoplasm]], near nucleus
- **Organize microtubules in the centromere**
- **Organize the mitotic spindle**
![[Centrioles.jpeg]]
## [[Chloroplasts]]
- Found in Plant Cells 
- **Site of [[photosynthesis]]**
- **Contains high levels of chlorophyll**
- **Converts sunlight energy into chemical energy** *(glucose)*
- Green
![[Chloroplast_II.svg]]
## [[Cilia]] & [[Flagella]]
- Found in [[1.1.4 -  Viruses vs. Prokaryotes#Prokaryotes|Prokaryotes]] & [[1.1.5 - Prokaryotes vs. Eukaryotes#Eukaryotes|Eukaryotes]]
- **Cell Movement**
- **[[Cilia]] move back & forth**
- **[[Flagella]] move like a propeller**
- Powered by [[Adenosine Triphosphate]]
![[Cillia-Flagella.jpeg]]
## [[Cytoplasm]] 
- Found in **all cells:** [[1.1.4 -  Viruses vs. Prokaryotes#Prokaryotes|Prokaryotes]] & [[1.1.5 - Prokaryotes vs. Eukaryotes#Eukaryotes|Eukaryotes]] 
- Majority of the intercellular environment
- Where metabolic reactions occur
- [[Dihydrogen Oxide|Water]], salts, & [[Proteins]]
![[Cytoplasm.png]]
## [[Cytoskeleton]]
- Found in [[1.1.4 -  Viruses vs. Prokaryotes#Prokaryotes|Prokaryotes]] & [[1.1.5 - Prokaryotes vs. Eukaryotes#Eukaryotes|Eukaryotes]]
- Cell Structure 
- Filament & Tubules in Cytoplasm
- Determine shape of cell
![[Cytoskeleton-1.png]]
## [[Golgi Apparatus|Golgi Bodies]] 
- Found in [[1.1.5 - Prokaryotes vs. Eukaryotes#Eukaryotes|Eukaryotes]]
- Process & package [[Proteins]] & [[Lipids]]
- Final folding of [[Proteins]]
- Directional
	- Cis Face — vesicles enter 
	- Trans face — vesicles exit
![[Golgi-apparatus-complex-modification-proteins-role-cell.jpg.webp]]
## [[Lysosomes]]
- Found in [[1.1.5 - Prokaryotes vs. Eukaryotes#Eukaryotes|Eukaryotes]]
- Digestive system of the cell
- Break down material brought into cell
- Break down worn out organelles
- Leads apoptosis
## [[1.1.6 - Plants vs. Animals#Mitochondria|Mitochondria]] 
- Found in [[1.1.5 - Prokaryotes vs. Eukaryotes#Eukaryotes|Eukaryotes]]
- Site of cellular respiration 
- Powerhouse of the cell
- Converts glucose to [[Adenosine Triphosphate]]
## [[Nucleus]] 
### Nuclear Envelope
- Found in [[1.1.5 - Prokaryotes vs. Eukaryotes#Eukaryotes|Eukaryotes]]
- Membrane around the [[Nucleus|nucleus]]
- Separate nuclear content, [[Deoxyribonucleic Acid]] from the [[Cytoplasm]]
- Two lipid bilayer membranes
	- Inner
	- Outer
### Nuclear Pore
- Found in [[1.1.5 - Prokaryotes vs. Eukaryotes#Eukaryotes|Eukaryotes]]
- Channel in nuclear envelope 
- Provides access to the [[Nucleus|nucleus]] 
- Regulate transport of [[Proteins]] and [[Ribonucleic Acid|RNA]] across the nuclear envelope
### Nucleolus
- Found in [[1.1.5 - Prokaryotes vs. Eukaryotes#Eukaryotes|Eukaryotes]]
- Located inside the [[Nucleus|nucleus]] 
- Produce and assemble the cells [[Ribosomes|ribosomes]]
- Ribosomal genes are transcribed
## [[Ribosomes]]
- Found in [[1.1.4 -  Viruses vs. Prokaryotes#Prokaryotes|Prokaryotes]] & [[1.1.5 - Prokaryotes vs. Eukaryotes#Eukaryotes|Eukaryotes]]
- Made of two separate subunits that come together
- Made of [[Ribonucleic Acid|RNA]] & [[Proteins]]
- Site of [[protein synthesis]]
- Converts genetic code (mRNA) into an amino acid sequence (protein)
## [[Rough Endoplasmic Reticulum]]
- Found in [[1.1.5 - Prokaryotes vs. Eukaryotes#Eukaryotes|Eukaryotes]]
- Helps in protein production 
	- Production
	- Folding
	- Quality Control
	- Dispatch
- Located right outside the nucleus
## [[Smooth Endoplasmic Reticulum]]
- Found in [[1.1.5 - Prokaryotes vs. Eukaryotes#Eukaryotes|Eukaryotes]]
- Helps in lipid production
	- Phospholipids
	- Cholesterol
	- Steroid Hormones
- Metabolism of [[Carbohydrates]]
- Located right outside the nucleus
## [[Vacuoles|Vacuoles]] 
- Found in [[1.1.5 - Prokaryotes vs. Eukaryotes#Eukaryotes|Eukaryotes]]
- Hold solution & materials in the cell
- Stores [[Dihydrogen Oxide|water]] and other materials
- Plant Cells - Large
- Animal Cells - Small
