- [[Proteins]] are made up of **[[Carbon]], [[Hydrogen]], [[Oxygen]], [[Nitrogen]],** and sometimes **[[Sulfur]]**

## Form follows function 
- The order of the amino acids determines the function/type of [[Proteins|protein]] 
# Functions
1. **Build tissue and muscle**
2. *Speed up chemical reactions* (**enzymes**)
3. Aid in **hormone production**
4. **Transport** molecules in and out of the [[Cell]]
