# Endosymbiotic Theory
- **ENDO**- *Within, inner, absorbing or containing.*
- **SYMBIOTIC**- *Interaction between 2 different organisms.*
- States some organelles in eukaryotic cells were once prokaryotic organisms. (Specifically the [[1.1.6 - Plants vs. Animals#Mitochondria|Mitochondria]] and [[Chloroplasts|Chloroplasts]])
