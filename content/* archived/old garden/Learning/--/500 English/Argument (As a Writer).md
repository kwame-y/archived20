# Argumentative Text Features
1. **[[Claim]]**: The writer’s main argument or position on the topic
2. **[[Evidence]]**: Factual Information examples or expert opinions that support the claim
3. **[[Counterargument]]**: An opposing viewpoint or [[argument]] that challenge the writer’s [[claim]]
4. **[[Rebuttal]]**: The writer’s main response to the counterargument, explaining why it is invalid or weaker than their own argument
5. **[[Logical Reasoning]]**: The use of sound logic and reasoning to support the claim
6. **[[Persuasive Techniques]]**: [[Rhetorical Devices]], such as emotional appeal or appeals to authority, used to persuade the reader
