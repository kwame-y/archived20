[[biology/botany/index|plants]] do this

## light-dependent rxn

$$
2 H_2O + 2 NADP^+ + 3 ADP + 3 P_i + photons → 2 NADPH + 2 H^+ + 3 ATP + O_2
$$

## light-independent rxn

$$
3 CO_2 + 9 ATP + 6 NADPH + 6 H^+ → C_3H_6O_3PO_3^{2-} + 9 ADP + 8 P_i + 6 NADP^+ + 3 H_2O
$$
