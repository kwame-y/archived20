## ornithology resources

- [Cornell Lab of Ornithology](https://www.birds.cornell.edu/)
	- [eBird](https://ebird.org/) - database of user-submitted bird observations
		- [maps](https://ebird.org/explore) & [datasets](https://science.ebird.org/) you can use w/o an account
	- [Merlin](https://merlin.allaboutbirds.org/) - app for bird sound ID
- [BirdCast](https://birdcast.info/) - migration maps
